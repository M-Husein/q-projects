2. Banknotes grade accepted by BRI

Banknotes Grade Details Exchange Rate Condition
1st Grade New series (above 2006’s edition); No fold and stain = Deal Rate
2nd Grade A Series (1996), B (1999), C (2001), D (2003), E (2004), F (2003A), G (2004A); A little fold and stain = Deal Rate – up to 10 points
3rd Grade Shabby, torn, fold and stain = Deal Rate – up to 100 points

3. Deal rate only applied for transaction above equivalent Rp 25.000.000,00 and with 1st Grade Banknotes. For transaction with 2nd Grade and 3rd Grade, please contact BRI’s branch near of you.

4. Customers are obliged to conduct money changer transaction to the Branch Office within 1x2 hours after the customers have booked. If within 1x2 hours customers do not make transaction, then booking will be canceled automatically.
